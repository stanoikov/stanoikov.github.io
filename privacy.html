To operate the gallery and player, count views, prevent abuse, comply with the law, and display advertising that helps keep the service running.

5. Data retention & removal

Uploaded videos remain available until removed by the uploader or by us. To request removal of content, use the Report a problem page.

6. Children's privacy

Vidaycoi is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
