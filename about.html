About Vidaycoi

Vidaycoi is honest video hosting for your own content. Upload an MP4, get a clean public watch page with a view counter, a shareable link, and an embed code — nothing else.

What we believe

A video host should get out of the way. That means no deceptive "unlock" gates, no anti-adblock circumvention, no auto-trimming, and no mirroring of other people's content. Advertising, when shown, is clearly labelled.

Only upload what you own

This platform is for content you created or have the explicit right to share. Our Terms forbid uploading other people's videos without permission, and our Report a problem page lets anyone flag content for removal.

DMCA & copyright

We respect intellectual property and respond to valid notices under the U.S. Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). If you believe content on Vidaycoi infringes your copyright, send a written notice including: (1) your contact information, (2) a description of the work and the infringing URL, (3) a statement of good-faith belief that the use is unauthorized, (4) a statement that the information is accurate and that you are the owner or authorized agent, and (5) your physical or electronic signature. We remove material that is properly noticed and may terminate repeat infringers.
